About Us

Welcome to ChicCrafts Boutique, your go-to destination for exquisite handmade beaded jewelry in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Founded in 2024 by Aishwarya Singh, we transform our passion for craftsmanship into beautiful, unique pieces that celebrate individuality and style.

Our Craft: Each piece is meticulously handcrafted using high-quality beads and sustainable materials. We offer custom designs, ready-to-wear collections, jewelry repairs, special occasion pieces, and workshops.

Our Mission: We aim to create timeless jewelry that makes you feel special and confident. Our commitment to quality, personalized service, and local craftsmanship sets us apart.

Our Services

Custom Jewelry Design

Personalized Creations: Work with clients to design unique, custom beaded jewelry pieces tailored to their preferences and occasions.

Consultations: Offer one-on-one consultations to understand the client's style, color preferences, and desired materials.

Ready-to-Wear Collections

Exclusive Collections: Showcase our range of ready-to-wear beaded jewelry, including necklaces, bracelets, earrings, and anklets.

Seasonal and Limited Editions: Introduce special collections for holidays, seasons, and special events.

Jewelry Repair and Redesign

Repairs: Provide repair services for damaged beaded jewelry, including restringing, clasp replacement, and bead replacement.

Redesign: Offer redesign services to update or transform old pieces into new, stylish designs.

Special Occasion Jewelry

Weddings and Events: Create bespoke pieces for weddings, proms, and other special events.

Matching Sets: Design coordinated sets for bridesmaids, prom groups, or other themed events.

Workshops and Classes

Advanced Classes: Offer advanced classes for those looking to improve their beading skills and techniques.
